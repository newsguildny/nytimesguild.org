Taking time off from work, even if you are unable to travel, is important for your
health and well-being. The pandemic has emphasized the need for employees
to step away from Zoom, allow time to recharge and maintain a work-life
balance. A number of people raised issues about their departments scheduling
the remainder of their 2020 vacation time.
